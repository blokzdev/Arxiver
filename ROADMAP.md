## Future phases (captured vision ‚Äî user-approved sequencing 2026-07-04)

> Strategic steer from the Co-Founder session of 2026-07-04, sequenced one-phase-at-a-time after
> the P-HTML close-out (device ledger burn-down ‚Üí M5 MathML verdict ‚Üí PH.8 go/no-go). Each phase
> gets the full Ultracode planning ritual + user plan-approval before code.

**P-Chat ‚Äî chat becomes a first-class surface.** The plumbing already outgrew its placement:
sessions persist + resume (Room), selection‚ÜíAsk lands in the paper's live conversation, collection
KBs work ‚Äî but history is buried under Settings and long conversations live in a cramped modal
sheet. Ship: a Chat destination in the bottom bar (or Library-adjacent), a full-screen conversation
view (the sheet stays for quick per-paper asks), a recent-conversations surface, pinned chats.
Medium cost, zero red-line pressure.

**P-Tools ‚Äî agentic tool-use: the corpus becomes searchable mid-conversation.** The
highest-leverage capture. Today retrieval is RAG-before-the-turn over what the user has; P-Tools
gives the model a tool loop ‚Äî `search_arxiv(query, category, date_range)` and siblings ‚Äî the
Claude-connector model, executed LOCALLY through the existing ‚â•3s limiter + AllowedHosts gate,
reasoned over by the user's BYOK provider, disclosed in the "what leaves the device" confirm
(extended to show tool activity). Turns Arxiver from "chat about your library" into a research
assistant over the living corpus. Cloud-first (Anthropic/Gemini tool APIs; LiteRT-LM Kotlin
tool support is an experimental flag ‚Äî on-device degrades gracefully to no-tools). **The
cross-disciplinary vision ships HERE as tools, not ingestion:** `search_chemrxiv` (Cambridge Open
Engage REST ‚Äî real search params), bioRxiv/medRxiv **via the Semantic Scholar bridge** (S2 already
indexes them and we already integrate it; bioRxiv's own free API has NO keyword/author search ‚Äî
date-range + category only), with one-tap import-to-library (PDF reading works day one). ~10% of
the ingestion cost for most of the discovery value. New hosts (chemrxiv.org if direct) are
red-line user-approval items at planning time.

**P-Sources ‚Äî first-class multi-source ingestion (demand-gated, v3-scale).** Full bioRxiv/chemRxiv
citizenship: follows/feeds, native reading, taxonomy. Requires the source-abstraction refactor
first (the app is deeply arXiv-shaped: `ArxivId` identity, seeded taxonomy, Atom client, and the
P-HTML reader is **LaTeXML-specific** ‚Äî other sources' HTML is a different markup family ‚Üí
per-source reader contracts, schema migrations). Do the abstraction as its own subphase, then
chemRxiv (searchable API) before bioRxiv (S2-bridge or date/category feeds). Gated on P-Tools
usage proving demand.
